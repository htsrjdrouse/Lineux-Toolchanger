Here are your steps to get started:
    1. Turn on powerpumpsraspi: powerpumpssocket start


    2. Turn on smoothiesockets: smoothiesocket start


    3. Turn on gearman socket: gearman start


    4. Turn on valve control socket: syringesocket start

    5. Turn on e1 temperature controller and headcamlinearactuator socket: headcamlinearactuatorsocket start


    6. Turn on power to motion controller: poweron




Here are your steps to stop:


    1. Turn off poweroff

    2. Turn off smoothiesockets: smoothiesocket stop


    3. Turn off powerpumpsraspi: powerpumpssocket stop


    4. Turn off gearman socket: gearman stop

    5. Turn off e1 temperature controller and headcamlinearactuator socket: headcamlinearactuatorsocket stop

    6. Turn off valve control socket: syringesocket stop
